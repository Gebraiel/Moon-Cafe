Moon Café was launched and started its actual activity in 2010 providing coffee products, drinks, sweets and sandwiches, and we have continued to develop and expand since then, as we aim to be the number one café in Saudi Arabia

We succeeded in record time because of our insistence on providing the best quality coffee at competitive prices. This proved the success of the cafe, a wide and comprehensive success in different parts of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, where we have more than 20 branches

We always strive to improve our products to make our customers happy, and our desire is to provide high-quality products. We always make sure that the products are from the best raw sources, and we are keen as much as possible to extract our products from natural sources and present them with love. We relied on choosing our location according to population density in groups, malls, hospitals and workplaces
